## RP2040 ![Raspberry Pi Pico with RP2040](images/pico.with.rp2040.jpg)
## Vlastnosti RP2040 I. * Dual-core Arm Cortex-M0+ @ 133MHz * 264KB pamäť SRAM * 16MB flash pamäť * DMA radič * Interpolator and integer divider peripherals
## Vlastnosti RP2040 II. * 30 GPIO pinov, z toho 4 analógové piny * 2 × UARTs, 2 × SPI, 2 × I2C * 16 × PWM * 1 × USB 1.1 radič a PHY, s podporou pre host a device * 8 × Raspberry Pi Programmable I/O (PIO) state machines * USB mass-storage boot mode with UF2 support, s možnosťou drag'n'drop programovania
