## Bluetooth Low Energy * known as _Bluetooth Smart_ * light-weight subset of classic Bluetooth * introduced as part of the Bluetooth 4.0 (2010) * the easiest way to design something that can talk to any modern mobile platform Notes: * https://learn.adafruit.com/introduction-to-bluetooth-low-energy/introduction#
## Device Roles * **Peripheral devices** - small, low power, resource contrained devices that can connect to a much more powerful central device * e.g. heart rate monitor, a BLE enabled proximity tag, etc. * **Central devices** - usually the mobile phone or tablet that you connect to with far more processing power and memory Notes: * https://learn.adafruit.com/introduction-to-bluetooth-low-energy/gap

## Advertising Process * **advertising packet** * send by peripheral device * bytes * retransmitted when advertising interval passes * **advertising interval** * from ms to secs * longer delays saves power, but feels less responsive Notes: * https://learn.adafruit.com/introduction-to-bluetooth-low-energy/gap
![BLE Connection](images/ble-connection.png) Notes: * https://www.slideshare.net/LarsAlexanderBlumber/ble-with-raspberry-pi
